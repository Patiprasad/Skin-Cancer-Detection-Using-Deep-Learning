Skin Cancer Classes:

AKIEC: Actinic keratoses and intraepithelial carcinoma (precancerous lesions).

BCC: Basal cell carcinoma (common, slow-growing skin cancer).

BKL: Benign keratosis (non-cancerous, includes seborrheic keratoses).

DF: Dermatofibroma (benign skin growth).

MEL: Melanoma (dangerous, fast-growing skin cancer).

NV: Melanocytic nevi (common moles, usually benign).

VASC: Vascular lesions (includes angiomas and vascular malformations).
